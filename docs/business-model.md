# Business Model: Independent Food Processing Operations

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-8160`
- ISCO-08: `8160`
- Occupation: Food and Related Products Machine Operators
- Social impact: local-jobs, food-safety, worker-safety

## Customer

- small food processors
- co-packers
- artisanal producers

## Offer

- batch process monitoring
- food-safety sampling
- allergen tracking
- line-clearance verification
- reporting

## Revenue

- monthly plant-operations platform fee
- per-batch fee
- reporting package

## Trust Controls

- no robot dispatch near cutting/mixing equipment without governor gate
- allergen cross-contact incidents cannot be suppressed
- batch and food-safety data is auditable, not editable
